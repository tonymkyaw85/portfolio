About Me

Hey there! I'm someone who loves to build things, especially in the digital space. I'm currently working on my web development skills. Think of it like this: I use HTML to create the basic layout of a website, like building the rooms of a house. Then, CSS is like the interior design, making it look good with colors and styles. I'm also learning JavaScript to add some fun interactivity, like making a light switch turn on and off.

Outside of coding, I'm really into photography. I love capturing moments and telling stories through images, just like I try to tell a story with a well-designed webpage. I also enjoy playing board games with friends; I find it's a great way to think strategically and have fun. I'm passionate about learning new things and collaborating with others to create something awesome. I'm eager to find opportunities to apply my skills and grow!
